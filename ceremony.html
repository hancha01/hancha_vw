In Collaboration with
TASTE OF SEOUL 2023

HANCHA has received an invitation to participate in "TASTE OF SEOUL" in 2023, an event organized by the city of Seoul and sponsored by Bar & Dining. TASTE OF SEOUL is a project initiated by the city of Seoul, South Korea, to showcase the diverse and distinctive culinary culture that Seoul has to offer.

Under the sponsorship of TASTE OF SEOUL, the "Seoul Food 100" is a project that selects 100 representative restaurants in Seoul across various categories, including Korean, Western, Asian, grill, vegetarian, cafe & dessert, and bars & pubs. This selection process takes into account factors such as globalism, expertise, and relevance.

On September 17, 2023, HANCHA will take part in the "Seoul Food 100 Award" event, where the selected companies for the Seoul Food 100 list will be introduced. Additionally, there will be a dinner party called "Seoul's Night," during which Damtuh will promote its tea products and the relaunched traditional tea brand HANCHA.
