In Collaboration With
TASTE OF SEOUL 2023

HANCHA has been invited to participate in "TASTE OF SEOUL" in 2023, an event organized by the city of Seoul and sponsored by Bar & Dining. TASTE OF SEOUL is a project initiated by the city of Seoul, South Korea, to showcase the diverse and distinctive culinary culture that Seoul holds.

Under the sponsorship of TASTE OF SEOUL, the "Seoul Food 100" is a project that selects 100 representative restaurants in Seoul in various categories, including Korean, Western, Asian, grill, vegetarian, cafe & dessert, and bars & pubs, taking into account factors such as globalism, expertise, and relevance.

On September 17, 2023, HANCHA will participate in the "Seoul Food 100 Award" event, which introduces the companies selected for the Seoul Food 100 list, and the dinner party "Seoul's Night," where Damtuh will promote its tea and the traditional tea brand HANCHA, which it is launching anew.
