CITRON GINGER

It is a tea that gives you comfort of mind and vitality with fresh citron and warm flavor ginger.

Soothe With Minds

Citron Ginger tea is packed with minerals and vitamins, known for its refreshing aroma. The combination of vitamin Crich citron and immunity-boosting ginger can revitalize your body. Find renewed vitality in your mouth with the invigorating scent of citron and the robustness of ginger. Enjoy a vibrant day with a cup of Citron Ginger.

* Add one tea bag to a cup of hot water(8 oz), let steep for 3-5 minutes and enjoy.
