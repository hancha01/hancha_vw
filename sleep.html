How to Enjoy:

  1. Pour 8 oz. freshly boiled water over 1 tea bag.
  2. Cover & Steep for 3-5 min.
  3. Squeeze tea bag to ensure maximum goodness.
  4. Enjoy 1-3 cups per day. Especially good with honey!
