Brand Story

HANCHA is a combination of 'HAN' which means Korea in Korean and 'CHA' which means tea.

We aim to pursue the taste and style of Korean tradition and provide good products to people all over the world.
We will provide brand value to provide Korea's unique beauty, Even a cup of tea, we will deliver the best value by considering the taste and health.

BI

The Brand Identity(BI) expresses the beautiful curved eaves of a
traditional Korean house(hanok) in the color blue, which is one of the five traditional Korean colors. Eaves function to enhance the safety and durability of buildings at the same time as beauty,
and the blue color of Korea contains positive meanings such as harmony with nature, peace,
and happiness.
HANCHA adds Korean flavor to this beauty and meaning of Korean tradition.
