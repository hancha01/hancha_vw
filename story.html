Brand Story

HANCHA is a combination of 'HAN,' which means “Korea” in Korean, and 'CHA,' which means tea.

We aim to pursue the taste and style of Korean tradition and provide high quality products to people all over the world.
We will deliver brand value by showcasing Korea's unique beauty. Even a simple cup of tea will be offered with the best value in mind, considering both taste and health.

Vision

We have the value of
providing the best service to
consumers with a cup of tea.

HANCHA aims to enhance the pleasure of purchasing and promote the taste and culture of Korean traditions to the world.

We are committed to providing the best service to our customers, one cup of tea at a time.
We strive to deliver the finest taste and quality by utilizing traditional ingredients that have been passed down through generations. HANCHA, crafted with the utmost care in blending,
will bring the beauty of taste to people all over the world.

BI

The Brand Identity (BI) expresses the beautiful curved eaves of a
traditional Korean house (hanok) in the color blue, which is one of the five traditional
Korean colors.
Eaves serve to enhance the safety and durability of buildings while also adding beauty.
The blue color in Korean culture symbolizes positive meanings such as harmony with nature, peace,
and happiness. HANCHA infuses the beauty and the meaning of Korean tradition with a touch of Korean flavor.
